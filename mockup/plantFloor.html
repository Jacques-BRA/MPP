Camera Re-Pack via ByVision · same camera as Assembly

⚖ ByVision

Non-serialized parts have no per-piece serial number, so the Sort Cage uses the same camera scanner as the Assembly Non-Serialized line to validate container trays. The camera scans the entire tray as a single image — one validation event per tray, not per piece. A container with 4 trays therefore yields 4 total passing scans. The PLC asserts TrayFullFlag at PartsPerTray; the camera fires its scan; on pass, the tray closes and a single ConsumptionEvent records the BOM consumption for that tray. Container fill is the MES-side accumulation of validated tray closes — same ByVision closure path as Assembly. Operator-rejected pieces (visual inspection before the part enters the tray) are routed to scrap before they ever reach the camera. No ContainerSerialHistory writes (those are serialized-only per UJ-05).
