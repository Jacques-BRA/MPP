# MPP MES — FDS Change Log (Pre-Release)

**Companion document to:** `MPP_MES_FDS.docx`
**Status:** Pre-release — change log lives here while the FDS is in active development. On customer-review release the change log will be reintegrated into the FDS itself.
**Maintained by:** Blue Ridge Automation

---

## Why this is a separate document

Until the FDS is released to MPP for customer review, every working session produces revisions worth capturing — but the cumulative change history rapidly outgrows what fits cleanly inside the design document. Pages of revision history bury the design content and make the FDS unwieldy.

We therefore keep two artifacts during development:

- **`MPP_MES_FDS.docx`** — the design document. Front matter shows the current version + date; no in-document change log.
- **`MPP_MES_FDS_CHANGELOG.docx`** — this document. The full revision history of the FDS through pre-release.

At customer-review release, this change log is reintegrated into the FDS in a tightened form (one entry per release, not per working-session turn) and this standalone artifact is retired or archived. From that point forward, the customer-facing FDS carries its own in-document change log.

## Convention

Newest at top. Each entry: **§ section(s) — driver/topic.** Specific FDS-XX-NNN req IDs created or rewritten. Companion data model / OIR / ERD versions where applicable.
